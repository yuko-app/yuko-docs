### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Widgets → Product Reviews Display**

![Prd Pn](/images/prd.png)

**Overview**

The **Product Reviews Display Widget** showcases customer feedback, ratings, and any uploaded photos directly on your product pages.\
It’s the most commonly used widget for product-level review visibility.

**Layout Options**

| **Layout**  | **Description**                                           |
| :---------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Grid**    | Displays reviews in a tidy, card-based layout.            |
| **List**    | A vertical feed, ideal for text-heavy reviews.            |
| **Mosaic**  | Staggered layout for a dynamic, media-rich look.          |
| **Minimal** | Clean and lightweight — shows only stars and review text. |

### **Style Options**

- **Card Shadow:** Choose _Dark_, _Light_, or _None_.
- **Card Corners:** _Sharp_, _Slightly Rounded_, _Rounded_, _Extra Rounded_.
- **Star, Text, & Background Colors:** Full control to match your brand palette.
- **Font Options:** Inherit your site’s font or set a custom one.\
  **Preferences**

| **Setting**               | **Description**                                     |
| :------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------- |
| **Visibility**            | Always Show / Always Hide / Hide if No Reviews.     |
| **Cross-product Reviews** | Display reviews from similar or related items.      |
| **Details Toggle**        | Show or hide review dates, variants, or pagination. |
| **Sorting Options**       | Sort by newest, oldest, highest, or lowest rating.  |

### **Advanced CSS**

Enable **Advanced CSS** to control fine details like padding, spacing, and hover effects.\
Perfect for developers and designers who want pixel-perfect consistency.
