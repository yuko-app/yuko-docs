### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Widgets → Star Rating**\
\
**Overview**

The **Star Rating Widget** shows your product’s average rating and review count, usually displayed under the product title or price.

This quick visual indicator improves click-throughs and buyer trust.

### **Options**

| **Setting**              | **Description**                                                |
| :----------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Show / Hide Elements** | Choose whether to display stars, count, or both.               |
| **Empty Star Display**   | Keep widths consistent across products by showing empty stars. |
| **Placeholders**         | {{count}} = review count, {{rating}} = average rating.         |
| **Style**                | Match color, size, and spacing with your product card design.  |
