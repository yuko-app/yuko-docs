### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Widgets → All Reviews**

### **Overview**

The **Wall of Fame Widget** compiles all approved reviews — across all products — into one grid or list.\
It’s ideal for testimonial or community pages.

### **Settings**

| **Setting**          | **Description**                                                |
| :------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Filter by Source** | Display only product reviews, shop reviews, or both.           |
| **Layout**           | Grid, List, or Masonry layout.                                 |
| **Media Display**    | Option to highlight reviews with photos or videos first.       |
| **Pagination**       | Choose to load reviews all at once or in infinite scroll mode. |
| **Custom Headings**  | e.g., “See what customers are saying.”                         |
