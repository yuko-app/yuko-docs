### **Summary: **

Make it easy for your customers to share their experiences with your brand. With Yuko, you can collect product reviews, shop-wide testimonials, and photo reviews directly from post-purchase emails or your on-site review forms.

### **Overview**

Collecting reviews is at the heart of Yuko.\
You can embed review forms on your product pages, send them in emails, or display a universal “Leave a Review” form on your site.

All forms are fully customizable — from layout and color to question types and rating styles — so every review feels native to your brand.\
\
**You Can Collect:**

- **Product Reviews** – Attached to specific items purchased.
- **Shop / Site Reviews** – Overall experience reviews for your brand.
- **Photo & Video Reviews** – Visual content to boost authenticity.
