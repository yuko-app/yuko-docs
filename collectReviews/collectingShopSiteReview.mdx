---

**Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → Reviews → Review Collection Form

**Overview**\
\
Shop reviews (also called **Site Reviews**) allow customers to rate their overall experience with your store — covering factors like product delivery, customer service, and checkout ease.\
 You can display these reviews in a dedicated “Store Reviews” section or on your homepage widget.**\
\
Settings**

| **Setting**           | **Description**                                                                        |
| :-------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Form Type**         | Select _Shop Reviews_ to collect general feedback instead of product-specific reviews. |
| **Rating Style**      | Choose from star ratings or emojis for a more engaging feedback experience.            |
| **Thank You Message** | Customize a confirmation message shown after a shop review is submitted.               |
| **Review Display**    | Display shop reviews separately or merge them with product reviews in widgets.         |

### **Tips**

- Use shop reviews to gather feedback on overall satisfaction.
- Combine product and shop reviews in your store homepage for stronger credibility.
- Enable **auto-publish** to quickly show positive feedback.
