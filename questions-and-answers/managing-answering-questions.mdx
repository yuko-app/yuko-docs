### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Questions & Answers → Manage Reviews**

![qayuko'.png](/images/qayuko'.png)

## **Overview**

From the **Manage Questions** panel, you can review, reply to, or delete questions — all from one screen.\
Each entry includes:

- Customer name and email
- Question text and date
- Related product
- Current status (Pending, Approved, Hidden)
- Replies and votes

  ## **Managing Questions**

  | **Action**       | **Description**                          |
  | :--------------- | :--------------------------------------- |
  | **Approve**      | Publish the question on your storefront. |
  | **Reject**       | Hides the question permanently.          |
  | **Edit**         | Update text or fix minor errors.         |
  | **Mark as Spam** | Filters out unwanted submissions.        |
  | **Delete**       | Permanently removed from your database.  |

  ---

  ## **Answering Questions**

  You can reply directly from within Yuko.

  **To reply:**
  1. Open **Manage Questions**.
  2. Click the question you’d like to answer.
  3. Type your reply in the **Answer Field**.
  4. Click **Publish Answer**.

  Your response will appear publicly below the question on your product page.

  **Next Step:** Continue to **Review Settings** — customize your review branding, moderation rules, and admin alerts.
