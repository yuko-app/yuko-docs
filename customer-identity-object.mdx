---
title: "Customer Identity object"
---

Customer identities represent individuals or entities in an external system and how they map to customers in a Yulko loyalty program. They contain basic details like email and name, along with the external system’s unique identifier for the individual. In practice, a single customer in Yuko may have zero, one, or many different customer identities.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customer-identities/customer-identity-object#schema-id)

**id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the customer identity in Yuko.

Example:

`863291377`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customer-identities/customer-identity-object#schema-first-name-one-of-0)

**first_name**

**string | null**

The first name of the customer in the external system.

Example:

`"Jane"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customer-identities/customer-identity-object#schema-last-name-one-of-0)

**last_name**

**string | null**

The last name of the customer in the external system.

Example:

`"Doe"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customer-identities/customer-identity-object#schema-email)

**email**

**string\<email\>**

The email of the customer in the external system.

Example:

`"jane@doe.com"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customer-identities/customer-identity-object#schema-distinct-id)

**distinct_id**

**string**

The unique identifier for the customer in the external system.

Example:

`"cust_19238475"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customer-identities/customer-identity-object#schema-properties)

**properties**

**object**

A restricted hash of additional attributes for the customer identity. Keys are allowlisted on a per-integration basis.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customer-identities/customer-identity-object#schema-customer-id)

**customer_id**

**integer**

The ID of the customer in Yuko that this customer identity is linked to.
