---
title: "Activity Object"
---

Activities represent actions performed by customers in the loyalty program. They are used to determine when rewards should be issued, track customer behavior, and personalize the customer experience.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/activity-object#schema-id)

**id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the activity.

Example:

`503256787`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/activity-object#schema-customer-id)

**customer_id**

**integer**

The ID of the customer who performed the activity.

Example:

`304169228`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/activity-object#schema-token)

**token**

**string**

A token identifying the activity that was performed.

Example:

`"activity_f57a9b5a8d0ac5"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/activity-object#schema-distinct-id-one-of-0)

**distinct_id**

**string | null**

A unique identifier for the activity in the external system (e.g. the order number).

Example:

`"212993"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/activity-object#schema-created-on-origin-at-one-of-0)

**created_on_origin_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time the activity was performed by the customer in the external system (e.g. when the action actually occurred, which is often earlier than when the activity is created in Smile).

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/activity-object#schema-created-at)

**created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the activity was created.

Example:

`"2024-05-15T08:35:08.920Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/activity-object#schema-updated-at)

**updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the activity was last updated.

Example:

`"2024-05-15T08:35:08.920Z"`
