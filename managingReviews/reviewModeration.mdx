Yuko includes built-in tools for moderation and quality control.

**Available Controls:**

- **Profanity Filter:**
  - Replace banned words with \*\*\*
  - Or hide reviews containing offensive content
- **Spam Filter:**
  - Automatically detects suspicious submissions
  - Keeps your store clean from bots or spam

**Navigation:**

\
**Yuko Dashboard → Settings → Reviews →General **
