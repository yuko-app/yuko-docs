Keep full control over every review your customers submit.\
In Yuko, you can approve, reject, reply, or tag reviews — all from one clean dashboard.

### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Manage Reviews **

![MANAGE Pn](/images/MANAGE.png)

### **Overview**

 The **Manage Reviews** page displays all customer reviews received through your store, whether collected via email requests or on-site widgets.

From here, you can view, filter, approve, or remove reviews — all in one place.

### **Review Status**

| **Status**   | **Description**                                                              |
| :----------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Approved** | Reviews that have been published and are visible on your storefront widgets. |
| **Pending**  | Reviews awaiting moderation or manual approval before going live.            |
| **Trash**    | Reviews that have been removed but can still be restored if needed.          |
| **Spam**     | Reviews flagged as spam or inappropriate — hidden from public display.       |

### **Review Filters**

Use filters at the top of the page to quickly sort reviews based on their status or interaction type.

| **Filter**     | **Description**                                          |
| :------------- | :------------------------------------------------------- |
| **All**        | Displays all reviews regardless of their status.         |
| **Approved**   | Shows only published reviews.                            |
| **Pending**    | Lists reviews waiting for moderation or action.          |
| **Trash**      | Displays reviews moved to Trash.                         |
| **Spam**       | Displays flagged spam reviews.                           |
| **With Reply** | Shows reviews that have received a reply from your team. |
| **No Reply**   | Shows reviews awaiting a response.                       |

### **Available Actions**

| **Action**  | **Description**                                                 |
| :---------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Approve** | Publishes the review and marks it as visible on-site.           |
| **Pending** | Moves the review back to moderation for further review.         |
| **Trash**   | Deletes the review from the active list but allows restoration. |
| **Spam**    | Flags the review as spam and hides it from display.             |
