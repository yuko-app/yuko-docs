title: "6.5 Product Blacklist"
---

Exclude specific products from being reviewed.\
This is helpful for items like gift cards, free samples, or discontinued SKUs.

**To exclude products:**

1. Go to **Settings → Reviews → Manage Exclusions**.
2. Under **Product Blacklist**, click **Add Product**.
3. Enter the Product ID or SKU.

Save your changes.
