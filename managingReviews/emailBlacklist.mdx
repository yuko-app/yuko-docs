title: "6.4 Email Blacklist"
---

Block specific email addresses or domains from submitting reviews.\
 Useful for excluding test accounts, internal users, or repeat spammers.

**To add to the blacklist:**

1. Go to **Settings → Reviews → Manage Exclusions**.
2. Under **Email Blacklist**, click **Add New Entry**.
3. Enter the email or domain (e.g., _@_[_example.com_](http://example.com)).
4. Save your changes.

You can also upload a CSV file to bulk import blacklisted emails.
