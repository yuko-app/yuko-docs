title: "6.3 Review Restrictions & Limits"
---

Control who can leave reviews and what they can submit.

| **Setting**                          | **Description**                                                   |
| :----------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Who can write a review**           | Choose between “Anyone” or “Verified Buyers only.”                |
| **Personal Information Restriction** | Automatically hides emails or phone numbers in reviews.           |
| **Maximum Upload Size**              | Limit photo uploads to 1 MB, 2 MB, or 3 MB.                       |
| **Review Quantity Limit**            | Set the number of reviews a single customer can submit per order. |

**Navigation:**

\
**Yuko Dashboard → Settings → Reviews → General **
