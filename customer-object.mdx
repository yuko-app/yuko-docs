---
title: "Customer Object"
---

Customers represent individuals in the loyalty program. They have a points balance, can earn points by performing activities, and can redeem points for rewards.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-id)

**id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the customer in Yuko.

Example:

`304169228`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-first-name-one-of-0)

**first_name**

**string | null**

The customer's first name.

Example:

`"Jane"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-last-name-one-of-0)

**last_name**

**string | null**

The customer's last name.

Example:

`"Doe"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-email)

**email**

**string**

The customer's email address.

Example:

`"jane@doe.com"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-state)

**state**

**enum\<string\>**

The customer's state in the loyalty program.

Available options:

`candidate`, 

`member`, 

`disabled` 

Example:

`"member"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-date-of-birth-one-of-0)

**date_of_birth**

**string\<date\> | null**

The customer's birthday. A year value of `1004` means only the customer's birth day and month was entered.

Example:

`"1004-05-27"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-points-balance)

**points_balance**

**integer**

The customer's current points balance.

Example:

`300`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-referral-url)

**referral_url**

**string**

The customer's unique referral URL. Used to share with friends as part of the referral program.

Example:

`"http://i.refs.cc/9qr5Pw"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-vip-tier-id-one-of-0)

**vip_tier_id**

**integer | null**

**deprecated**

The ID of the customer's current VIP tier. This field is now deprecated, use the nested `vip_status.vip_tier_id` instead.

Example:

`426715794`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-vip-status)

**vip_status**

**object**

An object containing details about the customer's status within the VIP program.

Show child attributes

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-created-at)

**created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the customer was created.

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/customers/customer-object#schema-updated-at)

**updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the customer was last updated.

Example:

`"2025-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`
