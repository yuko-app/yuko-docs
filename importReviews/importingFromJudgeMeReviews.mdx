### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → \*\*Reviews → Import → \*\*[**Judge.me**](http://Judge.me)

### **Overview**

If you’re migrating from [**Judge.me**](http://Judge.me), Yuko allows a quick and seamless import.\
All product reviews — including ratings, text, and media — are carried over to your Yuko dashboard.

### **Steps to Import**

1. Go to **Yuko Dashboard → Reviews → Import**.
2. Click \*\*Import from \*\*[**Judge.me**](http://Judge.me).
3. Upload your [**Judge.me**](http://Judge.me)\*\* Export CSV file\*\* (downloadable from your [Judge.me](http://Judge.me) account under _Settings → Export Reviews_).
4. Review your import summary and confirm.

Yuko will match products automatically based on SKU or Product ID.

### **What Gets Imported**

| **Data**                  | **Description**                                      |
| :------------------------ | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| **Reviewer Name & Email** | Preserved from your export.                          |
| **Product SKU / ID**      | Used to match reviews to products in Yuko.           |
| **Rating & Content**      | Full text and star rating values.                    |
| **Media**                 | Photos and videos, if URLs are included in your CSV. |
| **Dates**                 | Original submission dates are retained.              |

### **Important**

- Before importing, ensure your product SKUs match between [Judge.me](http://Judge.me) and Yuko to avoid mismatched assignments.
- Reviews that can’t be matched automatically will appear in the **Unassigned** section for manual review.

### **After Import**

Once your import is complete:

- Imported reviews appear under **Manage Reviews**.
- You can approve, edit, or reply to them as usual.
- All imported reviews are automatically tagged with “Imported from [Judge.me](http://Judge.me)” for easy tracking.
