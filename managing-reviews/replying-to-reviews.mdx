## Turn Reviews into Conversations That Build Loyalty

Responding to customer feedback shows you care and transforms one-time buyers into loyal advocates.

**What you'll achieve:**

- Strengthen customer relationships with personalized, thoughtful responses
- Resolve issues publicly and showcase excellent customer service
- Increase review response rates by 40-60%
- Improve customer retention by 25-35%

<Info>
  **Impact:** Merchants who reply to 80%+ of reviews see:

  - 30-45% higher repeat purchase rates
  - 50-70% improvement in negative review resolution
  - 35-50% increase in customer satisfaction scores
  - 40-60% boost in average customer lifetime value
</Info>

## How to Reply to Any Review

You can reply to both **Product Reviews** and **Store Reviews** directly from the Yuko dashboard. Replies are public and appear on all review widgets where the review is displayed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Where to Find Reviews" icon="gauge">
    **Navigation: Yuko Dashboard ‚Üí Reviews ‚Üí Manage Reviews**

    On the **Manage Reviews** page, you‚Äôll see two tabs:

    - **Product Reviews**
    - **Store Reviews**

    Both follow the same reply workflow.

    From this page, you can:

    - View all submitted reviews
    - Manage replies
    - Adjust review-related email preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Review to Reply" icon="message">
    Click on the review you want to respond to.

    - Go to **Reviews ‚Üí Manage Reviews**
    - Locate the review you want to respond to
    - Under the **review date** , click **Add Reply**

    ![Kml\\](/images/kml.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose Your Response" icon="pen">
    Enter your reply in the text box provided.

    After clicking **Add Reply**, a dialog box opens showing:

    ![Dia](/images/dia.png)

    - The customer‚Äôs review content
    - Product name
    - Review date
    - Customer details

    In the **Reply Content** field:

    - Enter your response to the customer
    - Replies are **public** and will appear on the review widget\
      \
      **Reply best practices:**
    - Address the customer by name when possible
    - Reference specific points from their review
    - Keep tone professional but friendly
    - Offer solutions for negative feedback
    - Express genuine gratitude for positive reviews

    **Character limit:** Up to 10,000 characters per reply
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview Before Posting" icon="eye">
    Review your reply for tone, spelling, and clarity.

    **Check that your reply:**

    - Sounds genuine and personal (not template-like)
    - Addresses the customer's specific feedback
    - Maintains your brand voice
    - Contains no spelling or grammar errors
    - Includes next steps if applicable (refund, replacement, contact info)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify the Customer (Optional)" icon="paper-plane">
    - Enable the **Notify customer** checkbox if you want the customer to receive an email notification
    - To use this option, make sure the **‚ÄúReview Reply Added‚Äù email template** is enabled in your email settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish the Reply">
    Click **Add Reply** to publish your response.

    Once added:

    ![Replu](/images/replu.png)

    - The reply appears below the review in the dashboard
    - The review list shows **Your Reply** under the corresponding review
    - The reply is visible on all widgets where the review is displayed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing or Deleting a Reply

You can update or remove your reply at any time.

- Click **Edit Reply** to modify the response
- Click **Delete Reply** to remove it completely

Changes are reflected immediately on the storefront widgets.

## Reply Best Practices

To build trust and improve customer experience:

- Address the customer by name when possible
- Reference specific points from their review
- Keep the tone friendly, professional, and on-brand
- Offer solutions for negative feedback
- Thank customers for positive reviews

> üí° **Tip:** Customers are more likely to trust brands that respond quickly. Consider enabling reply notifications to stay updated on new reviews.

<Note>
  **Timing matters:** 85% of customers expect a response within 48 hours. Set up reply notifications in **Settings ‚Üí Notifications** to stay on top of new reviews.
</Note>

## Reply Strategies by Review Type

Different review ratings require different response approaches for maximum effectiveness.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="5-Star Reviews (Positive)">
    **Show appreciation and build loyalty**

    **Reply objectives:**

    - Thank the customer genuinely
    - Reinforce their positive experience
    - Encourage repeat purchases
    - Invite them to share with friends

    **Example template:**

    ```
    Thank you so much for the 5-star review, [Name]! We're thrilled
    that you love your [Product Name]. Your feedback about [specific
    detail they mentioned] truly makes our day.
    
    We'd love to welcome you back soon! Use code THANKS15 for 15% off
    your next order.
    
    Cheers,
    [Your Name] at [Store Name]
    ```

    **Pro tips:**

    - Personalize by mentioning specific product features they praised
    - Offer a small discount or loyalty reward to encourage repeat purchase
    - Ask if they'd recommend the product to friends
    - Keep tone enthusiastic but professional
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="4-Star Reviews (Positive with Suggestions)">
    **Acknowledge positives and address concerns**

    **Reply objectives:**

    - Thank them for honest feedback
    - Address any concerns or suggestions mentioned
    - Show you're listening and improving
    - Invite them to try again

    **Example template:**

    ```
    Hi [Name], thank you for your thoughtful 4-star review! We're glad
    you enjoyed [positive aspect they mentioned].
    
    We've noted your feedback about [issue or suggestion]. We're
    actually working on [improvement/solution] and would love your
    input. Feel free to reach out at [email] if you'd like to discuss
    further.
    
    Looking forward to serving you again!
    [Your Name]
    ```

    **Pro tips:**

    - Acknowledge both positive and negative points they mentioned
    - Explain if there's a reason for limitations they noted
    - Invite direct communication for detailed feedback
    - Show you're taking action on their suggestions
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="3-Star Reviews (Mixed/Neutral)">
    **Turn neutrality into opportunity for improvement**

    **Reply objectives:**

    - Understand their specific concerns
    - Offer solutions or alternatives
    - Show commitment to better experience
    - Invite them to update review after resolution

    **Example template:**

    ```
    Hi [Name], thank you for taking the time to share your experience.
    We appreciate your honesty about [specific issue].
    
    We'd like to make this right. Would you be open to trying
    [solution/alternative]? Please contact us at [email/phone] and
    mention this review - we'll prioritize your case.
    
    We're committed to earning your 5-star experience.
    Best regards,
    [Your Name]
    ```

    **Pro tips:**

    - Don't be defensive - focus on solutions
    - Offer specific remedies (refund, replacement, discount)
    - Move detailed resolution to private channels
    - Follow up to ensure satisfaction
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="1-2 Star Reviews (Negative)">
    **Respond quickly, resolve professionally**

    **Reply objectives:**

    - Apologize sincerely for poor experience
    - Take ownership (even if issue isn't entirely your fault)
    - Offer immediate resolution path
    - Demonstrate excellent customer service publicly

    **Example template:**

    ```
    [Name], we sincerely apologize that your experience didn't meet
    our standards. This is not the level of service/quality we pride
    ourselves on.
    
    We'd like to make this right immediately. Please contact [email/
    phone] within 24 hours - we'll arrange [refund/replacement/
    solution].
    
    Your satisfaction is our priority, and we hope to regain your
    trust.
    [Your Name], Customer Care Manager
    ```

    **Critical rules:**

    - Reply within 24 hours (ideally within 12)
    - Never argue or make excuses
    - Take conversation offline for resolution details
    - Update public reply once issue is resolved
    - Learn from feedback to prevent future issues

    <Warning>
      **Avoid these mistakes:**

      - ‚ùå Blaming the customer or shipping company
      - ‚ùå Making excuses without offering solutions
      - ‚ùå Ignoring negative reviews hoping they'll disappear
      - ‚ùå Using defensive or aggressive language
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
