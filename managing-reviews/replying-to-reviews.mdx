title: "6.6 Replying to Reviews"
---

Engage with your customers by replying directly to their feedback.

**To reply:**

1. Open **Manage Reviews**.
2. Click the review you’d like to reply to.
3. Enter your response in the reply box.

   ![Replyingtoreviews Pn](/images/replyingtoreviews.png)
4. Click **Post Reply**.

Your reply appears publicly beneath the customer’s review.
