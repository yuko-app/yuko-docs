### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Workflows → Review Submitted & Reward Workflow**

### **Purpose**

Automatically thank customers when they submit a review and reward them with a discount or coupon.

### **How It Works**

**Trigger:** Review Submitted\
**Condition:** Review includes text, photo, or both\
**Action:** Send “Reward for Writing Review” or “Photo Review Reward” email

### **Setup Steps**

1. Go to **Workflows → Review Submitted & Reward Workflow**.
2. Select **trigger:** “When Review is Submitted.”
3. Add a **condition:**
   - Has Photo → send photo reward email
   - Text-only → send regular reward email
4. Choose your reward type (Fixed Amount, Percentage, or Free Shipping).
5. Enter the coupon details or integration source (e.g., WPLoyalty).

Save and activate the workflow.

### **Example Use Case**

- A customer submits a photo review
- Workflow triggers immediately
- Yuko sends a “Thanks for your photo!” email with a 10% off coupon
