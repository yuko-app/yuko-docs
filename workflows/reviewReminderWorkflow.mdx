### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Workflows → Review Reminder Workflow**

### **Purpose**

Remind customers who haven’t left a review to complete it.\
 This workflow gently nudges them with a second email.

### **How It Works**

**Trigger:** X days after “Review Request Email” sent\
 **Condition:** Customer has not submitted a review\
 **Action:** Send “Review Reminder Email”

### **Setup Steps**

1. Go to **Workflows → Review Reminder Workflow**.
2. Set the **trigger delay** (default: 5 days).
3. Choose the **Review Reminder Email** template.

Save and activate your workflow.
