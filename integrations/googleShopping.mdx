description: "Learn how to enable Google Shopping integration so your products display star ratings."
title: "10.7 Google Shopping Integration"
---

### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Integrations → Google Shopping**

### **Overview**

Push your product ratings directly to **Google Shopping** feeds.\
This helps you display aggregated review scores in Google product listings.

### **Setup Steps**

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Google Shopping**.
2. Connect your **Google Merchant Center** account.
3. Choose which products to include in the feed.

Enable automatic synchronization.

### **Notes**

- Google requires at least 50 reviews per store to show ratings.
- Ratings appear once Google approves your review feed.

Yuko automatically formats the data according to Google’s specifications.
