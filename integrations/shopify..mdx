### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Integrations → Shopify**

### **Overview**

If you use Shopify, Yuko connects directly through your store’s API — no coding required.\
This integration enables automatic order syncing, verified reviews, and review requests for your customers.

### **Setup Steps**

1. In your **Yuko Dashboard**, go to **Settings → Integrations → Shopify**.
2. Click **Connect Shopify Store**.
3. Log in to your Shopify admin account.
4. Approve Yuko’s access permissions.

Your store will sync automatically — including products, orders, and customers.

### **What You Can Do**

- Trigger automated review and reminder workflows after order fulfillment.
- Collect verified product reviews.
- Display product ratings on Shopify product pages.

Reward customers for reviews via discount codes.

\
**Note:**\
Shopify limits app permissions to certain scopes — ensure “read_orders” and “write_customers” permissions are enabled for full automation.
