### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Integrations → WPLoyalty**

### **Overview**

Reward your customers automatically for leaving reviews with **WPLoyalty**.\
This integration syncs Yuko review submissions with your loyalty and points system.

### **Setup Steps**

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → WPLoyalty**.
2. Click **Connect WPLoyalty Account**.
3. Log in with your WPLoyalty credentials.
4. Map your reward rules to Yuko events:
   - “Review Submitted” → Give X points
   - “Photo Review Submitted” → Give additional bonus points

Save and activate.

### **Benefits**

- Reward customers instantly after submitting a review.
- Encourage photo reviews with bonus points.

Sync point balances automatically across both platforms.
