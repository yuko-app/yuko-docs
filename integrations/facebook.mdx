### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Integrations → Facebook**

### **Overview**

Share your best reviews and testimonials directly to Facebook.\
This integration allows you to showcase real customer stories on your social media pages.

### **Setup Steps**

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Facebook**.
2. Click **Connect Facebook Page**.
3. Approve permissions to allow Yuko to post.
4. Choose your display format:
   - Review Card Post
   - Carousel of Multiple Reviews
5. Save and activate.

### **Benefits**

- Boost social credibility with real customer content.
- Drive traffic from Facebook directly to your product pages.
- Automatically refreshes with new 5-star reviews.
