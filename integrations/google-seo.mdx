### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Integrations → Google SEO**

![Ggds](/images/ggds.png)

### **Overview**

Display your product ratings and review counts directly on Google search results.\
Yuko automatically adds schema markup to your store pages, improving visibility and click-through rates.

## How to Configure Google SEO for Reviews

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Google SEO Settings">
    - Go to **Settings → Reviews → General**.
    - Locate the **Google SEO** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Google Review Rich Snippets">
    - Find the **Google Review Rich Snippets for SEO** option.
    - Toggle the switch **ON** to enable rich snippets.
    - Once enabled, a preview image will appear, showing how your product ratings and reviews will display in Google search results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="What This Does">
    - Displays **star ratings** and **number of reviews** in Google search results.
    - Helps customers quickly understand your product’s popularity and quality.
    - Improves **click-through rates** by making your product listing more visually appealing and informative.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tips for Best Results">
    - Ensure your product reviews are **accurate and up-to-date**.
    - Encourage **authentic reviews** to maximize credibility.
    - Regularly monitor how your rich snippets appear in Google search and make adjustments if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Benefits**

- Star ratings visible in Google search results.
- Boosted SEO ranking potential.
- Higher CTRs and conversion rates from search.

By using Yuko’s Google SEO features, your product reviews become more than just feedback—they become a powerful tool to drive more traffic and sales to your store.
