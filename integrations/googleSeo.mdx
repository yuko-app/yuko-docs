### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Integrations → Google SEO**

### **Overview**

Display your product ratings and review counts directly on Google search results.\
Yuko automatically adds schema markup to your store pages, improving visibility and click-through rates.

### **Setup Steps**

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Google SEO**.
2. Toggle **Enable Rich Snippets** ON.
3. Choose which product pages to include.
4. Save changes.

Google will automatically pick up your schema data on the next crawl.

### **Benefits**

- Star ratings visible in Google search results.
- Boosted SEO ranking potential.
- Higher CTRs and conversion rates from search.
