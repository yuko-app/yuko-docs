---
title: "4.6 Carousel / Testimonial Widget"
description: "Learn how to showcase customer testimonials in a rotating carousel on your store."
---

The Carousel Widget displays your best customer reviews in a rotating slider. It’s perfect for homepages, landing pages, and sales funnels where you want to add strong social proof without taking too much space.\
A well-placed carousel helps increase trust, highlight top feedback, and improve conversions.\
\
**How it looks**

You can add the Carousel Widget to:

- The homepage
- Landing/offer pages
- Testimonial sections
- Product or collection pages

The widget rotates reviews automatically or through arrows and dots, depending on your settings.

---

## Navigation

**Yuko Dashboard → Reviews → Widgets → Carousel**

From here, you can access all customization options and preview your changes instantly.

---

### 1) Widget Layout

Choose how the reviews should be arranged inside the carousel.

Options include:

- **Focused View** — Highlights one primary review
- **Vertical Sliding** — Reviews slide vertically instead of horizontally
- **Centered** — Centers the review cards for a balanced layout

These layout styles help you match the widget to your store’s design and available space.

---

### 2) Text

Customize the text labels displayed above or inside the carousel.

### **Header Text**

- Controls the title shown above the carousel

### **Out of Text**

- Appears after the average rating (e.g., “out of 5”)

### **Review Text**

- Controls how total reviews are displayed using the placeholder `{{n}}`

These fields allow you to match the widget’s tone to your brand voice or your store’s language.

---

### 3) Review Curation

Decide which reviews are included in the carousel.

### **Review Selection**

- Show recent reviews or refine manually based on your preferences

### **Minimum Rating**

- Only display reviews that meet your chosen minimum star rating

Use this section to highlight higher-quality or more relevant reviews.

---

### 4) Review Display Options

Control what information appears on each sliding review card.

Options:

- **Number of reviews to display**
- **Show reviewer name**
- **Show review date**
- **Show product block** (product image and title)

These options help you decide whether your carousel should be minimalistic or detailed.

---

### 5) Slider Arrows

Configure navigation and animation behavior.

### Options include:

- **Arrow position** — Choose where navigation arrows appear
- **Enable auto-play** — Automatically cycle through reviews
- **Show pagination dots** — Show or hide dots beneath the carousel

These settings control how customers move between reviews.

---

### 6) Design

Adjust the layout and structural appearance of the review cards.

### **Review Details**

- Show or hide the star rating
- Show or hide the verified badge

### **Dimensions**

- **Block height** — Sets the height of each review card
- **Page width** — Controls the carousel’s width relative to its container

### **Card Style**

- Show card shadow
- Apply rounded corners

These controls help the carousel blend smoothly with your theme’s visual style.

---

### 7) Pictures

Decide which images appear inside the carousel.

Options include:

- **Disabled** — No pictures shown
- **Show product pictures**
- **Show review pictures (if available)**

### Picture Physical Size

Adjust the pixel size for product and review images.\
Setting this to **0px** auto-optimizes the size for the best clarity.

### Advanced Picture Options

- **Prioritize reviews with pictures**

This section helps make your carousel more visual and engaging.

---

### 8) Colors

### Customize the colors used throughout the carousel.

### **Header Colors**

- Header text color

### **Widget Colors**

- Background
- Card background
- Card shadow
- Text color
- Rating icon color

### **Verified Badge Colors**

- Badge icon color

### **Arrow Colors**

- Arrow color
- Arrow background color

These controls ensure the widget perfectly matches your brand colors.

---

### 9) Advanced CSS

Add custom CSS to fine-tune styling beyond standard controls.

Options:

- **Enable Custom CSS**
- Add CSS inside the widget’s dedicated editor

This affects only the Carousel Widget, allowing targeted styling without modifying your theme.
