description: "Learn how to showcase customer testimonials in a rotating carousel on your store."
---

The Carousel Widget displays your best customer reviews in a rotating slider. It’s perfect for homepages, landing pages, and sales funnels where you want to add strong social proof without taking too much space.\
A well-placed carousel helps increase trust, highlight top feedback, and improve conversions.\
\
**How it looks**

You can add the Carousel Widget to:

- The homepage
- Landing/offer pages
- Testimonial sections
- Product or collection pages

The widget rotates reviews automatically or through arrows and dots, depending on your settings.

---

## Navigation

**Yuko Dashboard → Reviews → Widgets → Review Carousel**

![Carou Pn](/images/carou.png)

From here, you can access all customization options and preview your changes instantly.

---

### How to Customise Carousel Widget: 

The following options are available for customisation: 

### Layout

Choose how reviews move and align inside the carousel:

- **Focused View** – Highlights one main review
- **Vertical Sliding** – Slides reviews vertically
- **Centered** – Centers review cards for a balanced look

---

### Text

Customize the text shown in and above the carousel:

- **Header text**
- **Out of text** (e.g., “out of 5”)
- **Review count text** using `{{n}}`

---

### Review Curation

Decide which reviews appear:

- Show recent reviews or manually curate
- Set a **minimum star rating**

---

### Review Display

Control what appears on each review card:

- Number of reviews shown
- Reviewer name
- Review date
- Product block (image and title)

---

### Slider Controls

Adjust navigation and animation:

- Arrow position
- Auto-play
- Pagination dots

---

### Design

Style the structure of review cards:

- Show or hide star ratings
- Show or hide verified badge
- Set card height and carousel width
- Enable card shadow and rounded corners

---

### Pictures

Manage images in the carousel:

- Disable images
- Show product images
- Show review images (if available)
- Adjust image size (0px = auto-optimize)
- Prioritize reviews with pictures

---

### Colors

Customize widget colors:

- Header text
- Backgrounds (widget, cards, shadows)
- Text and rating icons
- Verified badge
- Slider arrows

---

### Advanced CSS

Enable Custom CSS to fine-tune styling directly within the carousel widget.
