### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Widgets → Carousel**

### **Overview**

The **Carousel Widget** is a rotating testimonial slider that lets you showcase your best reviews dynamically.\
Perfect for homepages, landing pages, or marketing campaigns.

### **Customization Options**

| **Setting**              | **Description**                            |
| :----------------------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| **Slides per View**      | Choose how many reviews to show per slide. |
| **Autoplay Speed**       | Adjust how quickly reviews rotate.         |
| **Transition Animation** | Fade, Slide, or Zoom transitions.          |
| **Review Filter**        | Choose top-rated reviews or specific tags. |
| **Navigation Controls**  | Add arrows or dots for manual scrolling.   |
| **Loop Option**          | Enable infinite rotation.                  |
