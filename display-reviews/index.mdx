Make your best feedback visible everywhere.\
Yuko lets you showcase reviews through customizable widgets — from product pages to carousels and badges.\
Each widget supports flexible layouts, styling, and filtering options so every display feels native to your brand.

### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Widgets**

### **Overview**

Displaying reviews builds trust and drives conversions.

With Yuko’s widgets, you can:

- Highlight your top reviews
- Display average star ratings
- Create a wall of fame or testimonial carousel
- Add floating badges to boost social proof

Every widget supports **custom colors**, **fonts**, **visibility controls**, and **advanced CSS** for precise styling.
