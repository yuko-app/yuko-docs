Review Display Controls let you fine-tune how reviews look and how customers interact with them. These settings apply globally to Product Review Widgets, Store Review Widgets, Review Collection Forms, and review request emails.

You can access these settings from:

\
**Yuko Dashboard → Settings → Reviews → General → Review Display Controls**

## **How Review Display Controls work**

These settings define how reviews are formatted and presented. Adjusting them helps you maintain consistent branding, improve readability, and guide customers toward writing better reviews.

Below is a full breakdown of every setting and how it affects your widgets.

---

### 1. Reviewer Name Format

Choose how reviewer names appear in your widgets.\
This helps you manage anonymity, privacy, and overall consistency across your store.

---

### 2. Date Format

Select how review dates are displayed anywhere a timestamp appears.\
Pick a format that aligns with your store’s locale or branding preferences.

---

### 3. Product Order for Review Collection

Decide how products are ordered in review request emails and in your review collection widget.\
Useful when customers buy multiple items and you want a predictable review submission order.

---

### 4. Number of Review Inputs

Control how many products appear in review request emails and collection forms.\
Adjust this based on how many items customers typically purchase per order.

---

### 5. Add Review Button Text

Customize the call-to-action label shown on your “Write a Review” button.\
Ideal for reflecting your brand voice or increasing engagement.

---

### 6. Photo Review Reminder Text

Edit the text used on the button that encourages customers to upload a photo.\
A simple way to boost the number of photo or media reviews.

---

### 7. Store Review Button Text

Change the text displayed on the button for submitting store-level reviews.\
This helps make your testimonial-collection flow clear and user-friendly.

---

## Tips for Using Review Display Controls

- **Match your brand tone:** Adjust button wording to reflect your store personality.
- **Encourage richer reviews:** Use motivating text to promote photo uploads.
- **Stay consistent:** Use a uniform date and name format across your store.
- **Optimize review collection:** Increase the review input count when customers purchase multiple items.
