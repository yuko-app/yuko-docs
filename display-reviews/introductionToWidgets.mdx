Yuko offers a collection of widgets that help you showcase customer feedback, build trust, and increase conversions.\
\
In this guide, we’ll walk you through each widget, what it does, and how it appears on your store — so you can create a smooth and trustworthy shopping experience for your customers.

### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Widgets**

![Y1 Pn](/images/y1.png)

### **Why widgets matter**

Displaying reviews at key stages of the buying journey increases trust and gives customers the confidence to complete their purchase.\
With Yuko’s widgets, you can feature your best feedback, boost product credibility, and highlight real customer experiences across your store.

All widgets include options for:

- Custom colors & fonts
- Layout & visibility rules
- Advanced CSS styling
- Responsive display across devices

### **Available Widgets**

<Accordion title="Product Reviews Display Widget" icon="sparkles">
  This widget shows all customer reviews on your product pages.\
  It usually appears below your product description.\
  Customers can read reviews, see photos or videos, and check the rating before buying.\
  \
  **Learn more**

  - _How to add the Product Reviews Display Widget_

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sidebar Widget" icon="sparkles">
  The Sidebar Widget is a small panel that stays on the side of your website.\
  It shows reviews on any page while customers browse.\
  This helps customers see social proof without leaving the page.

  **Learn more**

  - _How to install the Sidebar Widget_
  - _How to choose which reviews appear_
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Star Rating Widget" icon="sparkles">
  This widget shows the star rating and the total number of reviews for each product.\
  You can display it under product titles, on collection pages, or in search results.\
  It helps customers quickly compare products.

  ### **Learn more**

  - _How to enable the Star Rating Widget_
  - _How to show star ratings on collection pages_
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="All Reviews / Wall of Fame Widget" icon="sparkles">
  This widget shows all approved reviews in one place.\
  It can be used as a full page or a section on your site.\
  Customers can scroll through every review from your store.

  ### **Learn more**

  - _How to create a Wall of Fame Widget_
  - _How to customize the layout_
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Carousel / Testimonial Widget" icon="sparkles">
  This widget shows reviews in a rotating slider.\
  It is great for homepages, landing pages, or any place where you want to highlight your best reviews.\
  You can choose which reviews appear and how they rotate.

  ### **Learn more**

  - _How to add the Carousel / Testimonial Widget_
  - _How to select reviews for the carousel_

<Accordion title="Flowing Review Badge (Coming Soon)" icon="sparkles">
  The Flowing Review Badge is a small floating badge that shows recent reviews or rating highlights.\
  It updates while customers browse, giving real-time social proof.\
  This helps build trust without disrupting the page.

<Accordion title="Review Display Controls" icon="sparkles">
  These controls help you manage how reviews are shown.\
  You can sort reviews, filter them, hide low-star reviews, or pin important ones.\
