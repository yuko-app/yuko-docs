### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Emails → Review Request Email**

![Reviewrequest Pn](/images/reviewrequest.png)

**Overview**

This email is automatically sent after an order is fulfilled. It invites your customer to review one or more products they purchased.

Use this email to gather authentic reviews while your customer’s experience is still fresh.

### **Settings**

| **Setting**       | **Description**                                                                             |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Delay (days)**  | Choose how many days after fulfillment the email should send (e.g., 7 days after delivery). |
| **Email Subject** | Customize the title line for your request.                                                  |
| **Message Body**  | Add your message using personalization shortcodes.                                          |
| **Footer**        | Include signature, contact info, and unsubscribe link.                                      |

#### **Available Shortcodes**
