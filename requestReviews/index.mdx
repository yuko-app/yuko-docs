Automate how you collect customer reviews with beautifully branded review request emails.\
Yuko lets you send review invitations, reminders, and photo follow-ups — all fully customizable and connected to your store’s order data.\
\
**Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Emails**

![Yuko6 Pn](/images/yuko6.png)

### **Overview**

Yuko automates your post-purchase review flow so you can collect more authentic feedback with less manual effort.

You can send:

- **Review Request Emails** after order fulfillment
- **Review Reminder Emails** if a customer hasn’t responded
- **Photo Review Reminder Emails** for customers who left a review but no image
- **Reward Emails** to thank customers for photo or text reviews\
  \
  Each email includes flexible settings for **timing, content, rewards**, and **dynamic shortcodes** to personalize every message.
