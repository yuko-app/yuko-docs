If your customer doesn’t leave a review after the first request, Yuko can automatically follow up 

for you.

### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Emails → Review Reminder Email**

![Reviewrrr Pn](/images/reviewrrr.png)

### **Overview**

The reminder email serves as a friendly nudge for customers who haven’t responded yet.\
You can customize its timing, content, and design — keeping it simple and polite.

### **Settings**

| **Setting**               | **Description**                                                   |
| :------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Delay (days)**          | Number of days after the Review Request Email (default: 5 days).  |
| **Subject, Body, Footer** | Fully customizable through the email editor.                      |
| **Shortcodes**            | Same customer and order placeholders as the Review Request Email. |
