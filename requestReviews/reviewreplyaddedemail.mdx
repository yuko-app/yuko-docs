Keep customers engaged and appreciated by automatically notifying them when you reply to their review.

**Navigation**

\
**Yuko Dashboard → Reviews → Emails → Review Reply Added Email**

![Image9 Pn](/images/image9.png)

### **Overview**

When you respond to a customer’s review, **Yuko** can automatically send a friendly notification email letting them know that their feedback has received a reply.\
This builds stronger relationships, increases trust, and encourages customers to keep engaging with your store.**Settings**

| **Setting**          | **Description**                                                                                                                |
| :------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Notify After**     | Choose when to send the email — instantly, after 1 day (default), or with a custom delay.                                      |
| **Editor**           | Customize the **Title**, **Message**, and **Footer** of the notification email.  Example title: _“We replied to your review!”_ |
| **Reply Visibility** | Choose whether to show your full reply or only a short preview in the email.                                                   |
