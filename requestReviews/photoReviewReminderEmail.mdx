### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Reviews → Emails → Photo Review Reminder Email**

![Reviewphotoreminder Pn](/images/reviewphotoreminder.png)

### **Overview**

This email is sent to customers who have submitted a review without a photo.\
Encouraging photo uploads can boost authenticity and social proof across your widgets.

### **Settings**

| **Setting**         | **Description**                                   |
| :------------------ | :------------------------------------------------ |
| **Delay (days)**    | Days after review submission (default: 5 days).   |
| **Editor Sections** | Title, Body Message, Footer.                      |
| **Reward Option**   | Optionally include a discount code as motivation. |
