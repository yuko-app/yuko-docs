### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Questions & Answers → Widgets → Q&A Collection Form**

![Image31 Pn](/images/image31.png)

### **Overview**

The **Q&A Collection Form** is what customers use to ask questions about your store.\
It appears as a simple section or button below the product description — fully customizable to match your theme and tone.

**Form Structure**

| **Section**          | **Description**                                                                                                    |
| :------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Settings**         | General configurations for how the Q&A form appears and functions on your product pages.                           |
| **Questioner Info**  | Capture the customer’s basic details such as First Name, Last Name, and Email (private).                           |
| **Question Section** | Define how the question field appears — for example, you can customize labels, placeholders, and validation rules. |
| **Footer Config**    | Adjust the footer elements, such as disclaimers or “Powered by Yuko” visibility.                                   |
| **Thank You**        | Customize the confirmation message that appears after a customer submits a question.                               |
| Advanced CSS         | Add custom CSS for advanced design control and brand styling.                                                      |

**Form Preview**

The form begins with a short introduction section labeled **“About You”**, where customers enter:

- First Name
- Last Name
- Email (private)

This ensures each submission is associated with a valid customer identity while keeping privacy intact.

**Customization Options**

- Modify text labels, placeholders, and help descriptions.
- Adjust color schemes and button styles to match your storefront theme.
- Preview form design on desktop or mobile using the built-in toggle at the top.
- Save your settings to instantly apply them across your storefront.
