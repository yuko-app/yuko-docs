### Enable Admin Notification Emails

You can choose to receive email notifications whenever customers submit new content.

### To Enable Admin Notifications

1. Go to **Settings → Reviews → General**
2. Scroll to the **Admin Notifications** section
3. Enable **Admin Notification Emails**

Once enabled, additional configuration options will appear.

---

### Notification Email Addresses

Enter the email addresses that should receive admin notifications.

- Add up to **5 email addresses**
- Separate multiple emails with commas
- This field is **required** to enable notifications

Example:\
`admin@store.com, support@store.com`

<Info>
  Make sure all email addresses entered are valid to ensure delivery.
</Info>

---

### Notification Types

Choose which types of notifications you want to receive.

You can enable notifications for:

- **Product Reviews**
- **Store Reviews**
- **Questions & Answers (Q&A)**

You can select one, multiple, or all notification types depending on your needs.

---

### Save Your Settings

After configuring your preferences:

1. Review your email addresses and notification types
2. Click **Save changes**

Your admin notification settings will be applied immediately.

---

### When to Use Admin Notifications

Admin notifications are useful if you want to:

- Respond quickly to new reviews
- Moderate feedback in real time
- Stay informed about customer questions
- Monitor store reputation actively

For customer-facing emails, see **Email Settings** under **Settings → Reviews**.

---

### **How It Works**

Once enabled, the listed admins will receive an email whenever:

- A **new review** is submitted.
- A **new Q&A** entry is posted by a customer.

This ensures your team stays informed about all review-related activities in real time.
