Customize how your reviews look, behave, and are managed.\
The **Review Settings** area lets you adjust moderation rules, review limits, branding, notifications, and display preferences — so everything aligns perfectly with your store’s brand and policies.

### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Reviews**

![Image2 Pn](/images/image2.png)

### **Overview**

The Review Settings page is your central control panel for all review-related configurations.\
Here, you can define:

- How reviews are published
- What reviewers can submit
- How reviews are styled and displayed
- Which notifications you receive

![Image20 Pn](/images/image20.png)

### **Review Settings → General **\
\
\
**Detailed Breakdown**

Below is each setting explained in short, clear, help-center–ready descriptions.

---

### **Review Publishing & Moderation**

**Auto Publish New Reviews**

Automatically publishes all incoming reviews. When disabled, reviews stay pending until manually approved.

**Profanity Filter**

Blocks or masks reviews containing offensive or inappropriate words.\
Choose to replace bad words with asterisks or hide the entire review.

**Spam Filter**

Automatically filters out suspicious or bot-generated reviews before they appear in your dashboard.

---

### **Review Restrictions / Limits**

**Who Can Write a Review**

Choose whether anyone can write a review or restrict it only to customers who received a review request email (verified buyers).

**Personal Information Restriction**

Blocks customers from submitting sensitive details (like email or phone numbers) inside the review text.

**Maximum Upload Image Size**

Set the maximum file size allowed for photo uploads in customer reviews.

**Manage Exclusions**

Exclude specific customers or products from receiving review requests or writing reviews.

---

### **Review Display Controls**

**Reviewers Name Format**

Decide how reviewer names appear publicly (full name, first name only, initials, etc.).

**Date Format**

Choose how review dates are displayed on your storefront.

**Choose How Products Are Shown for Reviews**

Controls how products are sorted in review request emails and widgets (e.g., most expensive, alphabetical, latest added).

**Number of Review Inputs**

Set how many products appear in your review request emails or review collection widget (max: 5).

**Add Review Button Text**

Customize the text shown on the button that lets customers add a new review.

**Photo Review Reminder Text**

Customize the label of the button used to prompt customers to add photo reviews.

**Store Review Text**

Customize the text shown on the button that lets customers write a store-wide review.

---

### **Google & SEO**

**Google Review Rich Snippets for SEO**

Enable schema markup so star ratings and review counts can appear in Google search results for better visibility.

---

### **Admin Notifications**

**Enable Admin Notification Emails**

Turn on email alerts so the store admin is notified whenever a new review is submitted.

### **Admin Notification Emails**

Specify the email addresses that should receive new-review notifications.
