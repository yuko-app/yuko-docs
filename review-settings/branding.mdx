### What You Can Customize:

From the Branding section, you can configure:

- Review color palette and custom colors
- Fonts used in review widgets
- Star rating icon style
- Global branding application across widgets

These settings apply to all Yuko review widgets unless otherwise specified.

---

### Review Color & Style Settings

Yuko allows you to customize the colors used across your review widgets, including text, buttons, and star ratings.

### Available Options

You can:

- Choose a predefined color palette
- Customize individual colors manually
- Adjust button and input styles
- Control how ratings and descriptions appear

These controls help ensure your reviews feel like a natural extension of your storefront design.

---

### Apply Branding to All Widgets

You can enable **Apply to all widgets** to enforce consistent branding across your store.

When enabled:

- All review widgets will use the same branding settings
- Any widget-level color customizations will be overwritten

### Saving Your Changes

After making your branding updates:

1. Review your selections
2. Click **Save changes**

Your updated branding will be applied immediately to all applicable review widgets.

---

### When to Use Branding Settings

Use Branding settings when you want to:

- Match reviews to your storefront theme
- Maintain consistent design across all widgets
- Update the look of reviews without editing individual widgets
