## Build Trust with Overall Shopping Experience Reviews

Shop reviews (also called Site Reviews or Store Reviews) let customers rate their entire shopping experience ‚Äî not just individual products. These testimonials showcase your customer service, shipping speed, checkout experience, and overall brand quality.

**What you'll achieve:**
- Collect testimonials about overall shopping experience
- Showcase service quality and reliability
- Build trust with first-time visitors
- Highlight shipping, support, and brand values
- Display store-wide social proof on homepage

<Info>
**Impact:** Stores with shop reviews see 15-25% higher trust scores and convert 12% more first-time visitors
</Info>

---

## Shop Reviews vs Product Reviews

Understanding the difference helps you collect the right feedback at the right time:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Product Reviews" icon="box">
    **About specific items purchased**

    - Rating for individual products
    - Focus on product quality, fit, features
    - "This shirt fits perfectly"
    - Appear on product pages
    - Best asked 7-10 days after delivery

    **Goal:** Help shoppers decide on specific products
  </Card>

  <Card title="Shop Reviews" icon="store">
    **About overall store experience**

    - Rating for entire shopping journey
    - Focus on service, shipping, checkout
    - "Great customer service and fast shipping"
    - Appear on homepage/testimonial sections
    - Best asked after multiple purchases

    **Goal:** Build overall brand trust and credibility
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
**Important:** Don't ask for shop reviews in first purchase emails. Wait until customers have 2-3 orders or a great service interaction. Shop reviews work best from loyal customers who know your brand.
</Warning>

## How to Set Up Shop Review Collection

Yuko Dashboard ‚Üí **Reviews ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Review Collection Form**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Shop Reviews" icon="toggle-on">
    Navigate to the Review Collection Form settings and enable **Shop Reviews** as a collection option.

    **What this does:**
    - Adds "Leave Store Review" option to your site
    - Creates dedicated shop review submission form
    - Allows customers to review overall experience
    - Separate from product review forms
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Shop Review Form" icon="sliders">
    Configure the form fields and questions for shop reviews.

    **Recommended fields:**
    - Overall star rating (required)
    - Review text: "Tell us about your experience" (optional)
    - Reviewer name (required)
    - Reviewer email (required for verification)
    - Would you recommend us? (Yes/No toggle)

    **Optional fields:**
    - Shipping speed rating (1-5)
    - Customer service rating (1-5)
    - Website ease of use (1-5)
    - Upload photo (optional)

    <Tip>
    **Keep it simple:** Shop review forms should be even shorter than product reviews. 3-4 fields maximum. Focus on overall rating and open-ended experience question.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Shop Review Links to Your Site" icon="link">
    Place "Leave a Review" links in strategic locations.

    **Best placements:**
    - Footer: "Rate Your Experience"
    - Thank you page after checkout
    - Order confirmation emails (for repeat customers)
    - Customer account dashboard
    - About Us page

    **Link format:**
    ```
    https://your-store.com/shop-reviews
    ```

    Yuko provides embeddable widgets and direct links.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Display Shop Reviews" icon="browser">
    Choose where to showcase your shop reviews.

    **Recommended locations:**
    - Homepage hero section (testimonial carousel)
    - About Us page (trust section)
    - Footer (trust badges)
    - Landing pages (social proof)
    - Checkout page (reassurance)

    ‚Üí [Learn about display widgets](/display-reviews/introduction-to-widgets)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Encourage Shop Reviews from Loyal Customers" icon="heart">
    Strategically ask for shop reviews from your best customers.

    **When to ask:**
    - After 2-3 successful orders
    - After great customer service interaction
    - When customer contacts support with praise
    - 30 days after first purchase (for subscribers)
    - When loyalty program member reaches milestone

    <Warning>
    **Never auto-request shop reviews** to all customers like you do with product reviews. Shop reviews should be selectively requested from customers who have had enough experience with your brand.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Shop Review Form Customization

<AccordionGroup>

    - Universal, familiar format

    **Emoji Rating (5 emoji faces)** - More engaging
    - Sad to happy face scale

## Shop Review Collection Strategy

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Starting Out (0-50 Reviews)">
    **Best for:** New stores, just launching shop reviews

    **Focus:** Get your first 20-30 shop reviews from best customers

    **Strategy:**
    1. ‚ùå Don't auto-request shop reviews from all customers yet
    2. ‚úÖ Manually email 20-30 happy customers asking for testimonials
    3. ‚úÖ Add "Leave a Review" link in footer
    4. ‚úÖ Ask customers who contact support with praise
    5. ‚úÖ Offer small thank-you (5-10% off) for shop reviews

    **Expected results in 30 days:**
    - 15-25 shop reviews
    - 3-5% response rate when personally asked
    - Higher quality, detailed testimonials

    **Why this works:**
    - Personal requests get better responses
    - You control who you ask (select happy customers)
    - Early shop reviews set quality standard
    - Small incentive shows appreciation

    <Tip>
    **Template email:**
    "Hi [Name], we noticed you've ordered from us 3 times now! Would you mind sharing your experience with other shoppers? It only takes 60 seconds: [link]. As a thank you, here's 10% off your next order."
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Growing (50-200 Reviews)">
    **Best for:** Established stores, want more shop reviews

    **Focus:** Systematic collection from loyal customers

    **Strategy:**
    1. ‚úÖ Auto-request shop reviews from customers with 2+ orders
    2. ‚úÖ Add shop review form to "My Account" dashboard
    3. ‚úÖ Show "Leave a Review" prompt on order confirmation page
    4. ‚úÖ Ask for shop reviews 30 days after first purchase
    5. ‚úÖ Display shop reviews prominently on homepage

    **Expected results in 30 days:**
    - 30-60 new shop reviews
    - 2-4% conversion rate from requests
    - Mix of product + service feedback

    **Why this works:**
    - Targeting repeat customers (they know your brand)
    - Multiple touchpoints increase participation
    - Homepage display encourages others to review
    - Automated system scales with order volume

    <Warning>
    **Don't spam:** Only send shop review requests to customers who haven't recently received product review requests. Space them out (minimum 14 days apart).
    </Warning>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Maximizing (200+ Reviews)">
    **Best for:** Large stores, systematic testimonial collection

    **Focus:** Optimize collection and showcase best reviews

    **Strategy:**
    1. ‚úÖ Segment shop review requests by customer tier (VIP, repeat, new)
    2. ‚úÖ A/B test timing (30 days vs 60 days after first order)
    3. ‚úÖ Feature best shop reviews in marketing (emails, ads, social)
    4. ‚úÖ Create video testimonials from top text reviews
    5. ‚úÖ Use shop reviews as homepage hero content
    6. ‚úÖ Add shop review highlights to product pages

    **Expected results in 30 days:**
    - 80-150+ new shop reviews
    - 4-6% conversion rate (optimized)
    - Rich testimonials used across marketing

    **Why this works:**
    - Segmentation personalizes requests (higher response)
    - Testing finds optimal timing
    - Featured reviews encourage more submissions
    - Shop reviews become marketing asset
    - Multi-purpose use maximizes ROI

    <Check>
    **Pro move:** Turn your best shop reviews into case studies. Reach out to customers who left detailed positive reviews and ask if they'd be willing to do a short interview or video testimonial. Offer $50-100 gift card as compensation.
    </Check>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When should I ask for shop reviews vs product reviews?" icon="question">
    **Different timing for different feedback types**

    **Product reviews - Ask soon after delivery:**
    - 7-10 days after product delivered
    - Customer has used the product enough to review
    - Focus on product quality, fit, features
    - Send to all customers (automated)

    **Shop reviews - Ask after customer knows your brand:**
    - After 2-3 successful orders (repeat customer)
    - 30-60 days after first purchase (enough experience)
    - After great customer service interaction
    - When customer becomes loyalty member
    - Selectively to happy customers (not automated for everyone)

    **Can I ask for both?**
    Yes, but space them out:
    - Order 1: Send product review request (7 days after delivery)
    - Order 2: Send product review request (7 days after delivery)
    - Order 3: Send shop review request (focus on overall experience)

    Or:
    - Send product review request 7 days after delivery
    - Send shop review request 30 days after first order

    <Warning>
    **Don't overwhelm:** Never send product review request and shop review request in the same week. Space them at least 14 days apart to avoid review fatigue.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Should shop reviews be separate from product reviews?" icon="split">
    **It depends on your display strategy**

    **Option 1: Keep them completely separate** (recommended)
    - Shop reviews appear only in testimonial sections
    - Product reviews appear only on product pages
    - Clear distinction for customers
    - Easier to manage and display
    - Less confusing

    **Where shop reviews go:**
    - Homepage testimonial carousel
    - About Us page
    - Dedicated shop reviews page
    - Footer trust section
    - Checkout reassurance

    **Where product reviews go:**
    - Individual product pages
    - Product listing grids
    - Review widgets throughout site

    **Option 2: Combine them in some locations**
    - Show both on homepage (mixed testimonials)
    - Filter option: "Shop Reviews" vs "Product Reviews"
    - All reviews on dedicated reviews page
    - More content, but requires clear labeling

    **Recommended approach:** Keep them separate for clarity. Shop reviews are testimonials about your brand, product reviews are buying guides for specific items. Different purposes, different placements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get customers to leave detailed shop reviews?" icon="pen">
    **Most shop reviews are too brief - here's how to get detailed testimonials:**

    **1. Ask specific questions in the form:**
    Instead of: "How was your experience?"
    Ask: "What did you love most about shopping with us?"

    Other good questions:
    - "How did our product/service help you?"
    - "What made you choose us over other stores?"
    - "How was your shipping and customer service experience?"
    - "What would you tell a friend about us?"

    **2. Show examples of good reviews:**
    Display 2-3 detailed review examples above the form:
    - "Check out what Sarah shared about her experience"
    - Shows customers what "good" looks like
    - Increases average review length by 40%

    **3. Offer incentive for detailed reviews:**
    "Leave a detailed review (50+ words) and get 15% off your next order"
    - Longer reviews only (system can count words)
    - Better quality feedback
    - 2-3x more detailed testimonials

    **4. Follow up with top customers personally:**
    Email: "Hi [Name], we'd love to feature your story. Would you be willing to share more about your experience with us? We can send you a few questions, and as a thank you, here's a $25 gift card."
    - Personal outreach gets best testimonials
    - Can turn into case study or video
    - Shows you value their feedback

    <Tip>
    **Pro tip:** The best shop reviews come from conversations, not forms. When a customer emails you with praise, reply: "Thank you! Would you mind sharing this on our reviews page? [link]. Your words would really help other shoppers discover us."
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
