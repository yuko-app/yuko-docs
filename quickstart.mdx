## Getting Started with Yuko

\
Yuko is a Retention CRM built for eCommerce stores. You can use Yuko to manage **Reviews, Loyalty, Referrals, and Memberships** and strengthen customer relationships.

This guide will help you get started with Yuko and walk you through the essential areas of the app.

## **Modules in Yuko**

You can enable each module individually based on your business needs:

- **Reviews**
- **Loyalty**
- **Referrals**
- **Memberships**

## **Using Yuko Reviews**

Yuko Reviews allows you to collect, manage, and display product and site reviews, as well as handle customer questions.
