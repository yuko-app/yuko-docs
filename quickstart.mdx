## Getting Started with Yuko

\
Yuko is a Retention CRM built for eCommerce stores. You can use Yuko to manage **Reviews, Loyalty, Referrals, and Memberships** and strengthen customer relationships.

This article outlines the basics of getting started.

## **Modules in Yuko**

You can enable each module individually based on your business needs:

- **Reviews**
- **Loyalty**
- **Referrals**
- **Memberships**
