### **Request Reviews**

Use Yuko to send automated messages to customers after purchase:

- Review request emails
- Reminder emails
- Rewards for completed reviews
- Automated workflows for sending requests and reminders

### **Collect Reviews**

Collect reviews through:

- The Review Collection Form
- Automated review request emails

### **Display Reviews**

Display customer feedback across your storefront using:

- Product review widgets
- Star ratings
- Review carousels

### **Manage Reviews**

From the Reviews dashboard, you can:

- Approve or reject reviews
- Publish reviews
- Manage product questions and responses

## **Advanced Workflows**

Advanced Workflows help you automate review collection by triggering:

- Review requests
- Review reminders
- Review rewards

## **Next Steps**

Follow these steps to get started with Yuko Reviews:

1. **Ask Reviews** – Customize and activate review request emails.
2. **Collect Reviews** – Enable your review collection form.
3. **Display Reviews** – Add a product review widget, carousel, or star rating to your store.
4. **Manage Reviews** – Moderate and publish new reviews, or automate approvals.
