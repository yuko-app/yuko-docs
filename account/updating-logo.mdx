### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Account → Branding**

### **Overview**

Your logo is automatically used across your review request emails, review forms, and widget headers.\
This helps maintain visual consistency and build customer trust.

### **Steps to Upload or Update Your Logo**

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Branding**.
2. Click **Upload Logo**.
3. Select your image file (JPG, PNG, or SVG supported).
4. Adjust sizing or cropping if needed.

Click **Save** to confirm changes.

\
**Recommended Image Specs**

| **Type**         | **Recommended Size** | **Format** |
| :--------------- | :------------------- | :--------- |
| **Primary Logo** | 400×120 px           | PNG or SVG |
| **Email Logo**   | 200×60 px            | PNG        |
| **Widget Logo**  | Auto-resized         | PNG or SVG |

**Tip:**\
Use a transparent PNG or SVG logo to ensure it looks crisp on both light and dark themes.
