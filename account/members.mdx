### **Navigation**

**Yuko Dashboard → Settings → Account → Members**

![YUKO 01 17 2026 12 35 AM](/images/YUKO-01-17-2026_12_35_AM.png)

### **Overview**

Invite and manage your team members who collaborate on reviews, workflows, and email templates. Each member has a defined role that controls their level of access within your Yuko workspace.

### **Members List**

You can view all active members along with their **Name** and **Email Address**
