### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → Settings → Account → Members

![mem.png](/images/mem.png)

### **Overview**

Invite and manage your team members who collaborate on reviews, workflows, and email templates. Each member has a defined role that controls their level of access within your Yuko workspace.

### **Members List**

You can view all active members along with their **Name**, **Email**, and **Role**.\
Admins can also **Reset Passwords** directly from this section for any member listed.

### **User Roles**

| **Role**   | **Permissions**                                                                                |
| :--------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Admin**  | Full access — can manage settings, workflows, members, and organizations.                      |
| **Editor** | Can create and edit workflows, emails, and widgets but cannot modify billing or member access. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access — can view analytics and reviews but cannot make edits.                       |

### **Additional Option**

Members with Admin privileges can also **add a new organization** directly from this screen.
