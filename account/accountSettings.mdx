### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Account → General**

![Image8 Pn](/images/image8.png)

**Overview**

Manage your organization’s global settings to ensure consistent communication and accurate time preferences.\
From email sender details to time zone configuration, these settings apply across all Yuko emails and activities.

### **Available Settings**

| **Setting**     | **Description**                                                                                                 |
| :-------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Timezone**    | Choose your preferred time zone for all application activities and email scheduling.                            |
| **Date Format** | Select how dates are displayed (e.g., MM/DD/YYYY).                                                              |
| **Time Format** | Choose between a 12-hour or **24-hour** time display.                                                           |
| **From Name**   | Define the sender name that appears in outgoing emails (e.g., “Yuko” or your brand name).                       |
| **Reply Email** | Specify the default “Reply-To” email address for responses (e.g., [noreply@yuko.com](mailto:noreply@yuko.com)). |
| **Logo**        | Upload your company logo to appear in emails and other communications.                                          |

### **How to Update**

1. Go to **Settings → Account → General**.
2. Adjust the desired fields — timezone, formats, or email identity.
3. Upload your logo if needed.
4. Click **Save Changes** to apply your updates.
