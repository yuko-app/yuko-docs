### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → Settings → Account → Security

### **Overview**

Manage your account’s login credentials and keep your Yuko workspace secure.\
 You can update your registered email address and change your password anytime to maintain account safety.

### **Available Options**

| **Setting**          | **Description**                                                                                        |
| :------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Email**            | Update the email address associated with your Yuko account. Click **Update Email** to save the change. |
| **Current Password** | Enter your existing password to authorize updates.                                                     |
| **New Password**     | Create a new secure password for your account.                                                         |
| **Confirm Password** | Re-enter the new password to confirm the change.                                                       |
| **Update Password**  | Click to apply your new password. You’ll need to log in again using the updated credentials.           |

### **Note**

Changing your password will automatically log you out. You’ll need to re-login using the new password to continue accessing your Yuko dashboard.
