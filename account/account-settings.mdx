### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Account → General**

![Ge](/images/ge.png)

**Overview**

Manage your organization’s global settings to ensure consistent communication and accurate time preferences.\
From email sender details to time zone configuration, these settings apply across all Yuko emails and activities.

### **Available Settings**

| **Setting**     | **Description**                                                                                                 |
| :-------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Timezone**    | Choose your preferred time zone for all application activities and email scheduling.                            |
| **Date Format** | Select how dates are displayed (e.g., MM/DD/YYYY).                                                              |
| **Time Format** | Choose between a 12-hour or **24-hour** time display.                                                           |
| **From Name**   | Define the sender name that appears in outgoing emails (e.g., “Yuko” or your brand name).                       |
| **Reply Email** | Specify the default “Reply-To” email address for responses (e.g., [noreply@yuko.com](mailto:noreply@yuko.com)). |
| **Logo**        | Upload your company logo to appear in emails and other communications.                                          |

### **Accessing Account Settings**

1. Log in to your **Yuko Dashboard**.
2. Navigate to **Settings → Account Settings → General**.

### **Configurable Fields**

- **Time Zone:**\
  Select your local time zone. This ensures timestamps on reviews, notifications, and dashboards are accurate.
- **Date Format:**\
  Choose your preferred format (e.g., `DD/MM/YYYY` or `MM/DD/YYYY`). This format will be used throughout your dashboard and in exported reports.
- **Time Format:**\
  Select between **12-hour** or **24-hour** formats for displaying time.
- **From Name:**\
  The default name displayed when Yuko sends emails to customers. Example: “Your Store Team.”
- **Reply Email:**\
  The default email address customers can reply to for review notifications.

### **Tips**

- Keep your **From Name and Reply Email** consistent with your store branding to improve open rates.
- If you manage multiple time zones, ensure your **Time Zone** reflects your primary operational region.
