Getting Yuko up and running is straightforward. Learn how to install and connect Yuko to your store.

### **1. Create Your Account**

1. Go to[ <u>https://app.yukoapp.com/</u>](https://app.yukoapp.com/) and click Sign Up.
2. Enter your name, email, and password to create your Yuko workspace.
3. This workspace is where you’ll build widgets, customize emails, and manage settings.

### **2) Verify Your Email**

1. Check your inbox for the verification email.
2. Click the link inside to confirm and activate your account.

### **3) Add Your Organization Details**

Once logged in, go to **Settings → Organization** and fill in:

- Company Name
- Address
- City, State, Country, ZIP
- Phone Number

Click **Save Details** to continue.

### **4) Download & Install the Plugin**

1. From your Yuko Dashboard, download the Yuko Plugin.
2. Log in to your WordPress Admin Panel.
3. Go to Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin.
4. Upload the [Yuko.zip](http://Yuko.zip) file.
5. Click Install, then Activate.

You’ll now see Yuko in your WordPress sidebar.

### **5) Connect Your Store**

1. In your Yuko Dashboard, go to Settings → Integrations.
2. Enter your Store URL.
3. Click Connect Store to authorize the integration.

Once connected, your store’s products, orders, and customers will sync automatically.
