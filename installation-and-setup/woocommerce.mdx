## Before You Start

This guide helps you install Yuko on your WordPress + WooCommerce store and connect it to start collecting customer reviews.

**What you'll achieve:**

- Install and activate Yuko plugin on your WordPress site
- Connect your WooCommerce store to Yuko Dashboard
- Sync your products, orders and customers automatically
- Ready to send review requests and display reviews

<Info>
  **Total time needed:** 15-20 minutes **Technical level:** Beginner-friendly (no coding required)
</Info>

---

## Requirements Checklist

Before starting, make sure you have:

<Check>
  **WordPress Requirements:**

  - WordPress version 5.8 or higher
  - WooCommerce plugin installed and active (version 6.0+)
  - PHP version 7.4 or higher
  - Admin access to your WordPress dashboard
</Check>

<Tip>
  **Check your versions:** Go to WordPress Admin → Dashboard (top right corner shows WordPress version) Go to WooCommerce → Status → System Status Report (shows WooCommerce and PHP versions)
</Tip>

<Check>
  **What you need ready:**

  - Your business email address
  - Store name and website URL
  - Company information (name, address, phone)
  - Access to your email inbox (for verification)
</Check>

## Installation Steps

Follow these 5 steps to complete your Yuko installation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Yuko Account" icon="user-plus">
    First, create your Yuko account where you'll manage all review settings.

    **How to create account:**

    1. Visit [app.yukoapp.com](https://app.yukoapp.com/)
    3. Enter your information:
       - Full name
       - Business email address (use your store's domain email if possible)
       - Create a strong password
    4. Click the **"Sign up"** button

    ![Yuko1 Pn](/images/yuko1.png)

    **What happens:** Yuko creates your workspace (Dashboard) where you'll manage reviews, emails, and settings.

    **Time:** 2 minutes

    <Warning>
      **Important:** Use a business email, not a personal Gmail/Yahoo. Some features may require a verified business domain.
    </Warning>
    ---

    ### Verify Your Email

    - Check your inbox for the Yuko verification email
    - Click the verification link to activate your account
    - If not received, check spam or use **Resend verification email**

    Once done, you will be redirected to the Yuko Dashboard to continue the next steps.

    ![Yuko2 Pn](/images/yuko2.png)

    <Tip>
      **Email not received?**

      - Check your spam/junk folder
      - Wait 2-3 minutes (sometimes delayed)
      - Click "Resend verification email" on the Yuko page
      - Make sure you entered the correct email address
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Business Information" icon="building">
    Tell Yuko about your business. This information appears in review request emails and helps with compliance.

    1. Fill in all required fields:

    - **Business Name and Email Address** - Your store name (as customers know it) and the corresponding email address
    - **Address** - Your business address
    - **City, State, Country** - Location details
    - **ZIP/Postal Code** - Your postal code
    - **Phone Number** - Customer support phone number
    - Click "**Update**".

    ![Yuko3 Pn](/images/yuko3.png)

    **Why this matters:** This information shows in your review request email footers (required by email regulations in many countries).

    **Time:** 3 minutes

    <Info>
      **Privacy note:** Your customers see this information in review emails. Use your public business address, not a personal home address.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the Yuko Plugin" icon="download">
    Download the plugin file that connects your WordPress site to Yuko.

    **How to download:**

    - Download the Yuko WooCommerce plugin when prompted by clicking on **"Download Plugin"**
    - You will see a zip file downloaded
    - Upload and activate it on your WordPress site

    ![Yuko4 Pn](/images/yuko4.png)

    **What you downloaded:** A ZIP file (approximately 5 MB) containing the Yuko WordPress plugin.

    **Time:** 1 minute

    <Tip>
      **File location:** Remember where you saved yuko.zip - you'll need it in the next step. Most browsers save to your Downloads folder.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Plugin on WordPress" icon="plug">
    Upload and activate the Yuko plugin in your WordPress admin panel.

    **How to install:**

    1. Open a new browser tab
    2. Log in to your **WordPress Admin Panel** (usually: yoursite.com/wp-admin)
    3. In the left sidebar, go to **Plugins → Add New**
    4. Click the **Upload Plugin** button (at the top of the page)
    5. Click **Choose File**
    6. Select the `yuko.zip` file you downloaded
    7. Click **Install Now**
    8. Wait for the progress bar to complete (30-60 seconds)
    9. Click **Activate Plugin**

    ![Yuko5 1 Pn](/images/yuko5.1.png)

    **Success indicator:** You'll see "Plugin activated" message, and "Yuko Integration" appear in your WordPress sidebar.

    **Time:** 3 minutes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Store to Yuko" icon="link">
    Link your WooCommerce store with Yuko so they can communicate.

    **How to connect:**\
    \
    Return to your **Yuko Onboarding** (you will be redirected to this page)

    4. In the **Store URL** field, enter your store's URL
       - Example: `https://yourstore.com` or `https://shop.yourstore.com`
       - Include `https://` but not `/wp-admin` or other paths
    7. Click **Authenticate** to give Yuko permission

       ![Importa](/images/importa.png)
    8. Wait for the connection to complete (shows "Connected" status)

       ![Sett](/images/sett.png)

    ---

    ### Sync Your Store Data

    After connecting, manually sync your products and customers:

    1. In **Yuko Dashboard**, stay in **Settings → Integrations**
    2. Find the **WooCommerce** platform integration
    3. Click the **Configure** button
    4. Click **Sync Data** button to start importing:
       - All products from your catalog
       - Customer list (names and emails only)
       - Existing WooCommerce reviews (if any)
    5. Wait for sync to complete

    **Sync time:** 1-5 minutes depending on store size (small stores sync in 1 minute, large stores may take 5 minutes)

    **Total time for this step:** 7 minutes (connection + sync)

    <Info>
      **Installation Complete!** Your WooCommerce store is now connected to Yuko and ready to collect reviews.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
